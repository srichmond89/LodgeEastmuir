MyBathroom is a partnership run company. We are trusted tradesmen, with over 15 years experience in the bathroom industry. It gives our customers all the peace of mind, knowing its been carried out correctly, in a professional manner and most importantly on time. Throughout the process of the survey, designing and installation is delivered to the highest standard. We use high quality products so we can deliver the bathroom of your dreams. Our services are second to none. We always believe that customers should trust a tradesman not a salesman.

Take a look at some of the services that we can provide.

Supply & design

As a supply service we aim to deliver your desired bathroom, we design to suit our customers needs and with our skills to deliver that dream bathroom

Wet room installation

We specialise in designing and installation of wet rooms with full waterproofing as standard

Wetwall installation

We also give our customers the added option of wetwall installations to give your bathroom an ultra sleek finish

All Trades

We also cover all trades, so we can cover kitchen installations, boiler and heating systems to full tiles services and electrical work
